Goat cheese or Beetroot Salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce/Any Leafy salad based on what you like.

  • Beetroot(cut into pieces).

  • Goats Cheese

  • Walnuts(Crush up abit)

  • Balsamic Dressing(you can also make your own if you know how)

Method

  1. Put Lettuce/leafy salad on a plate.

  2. Add Beetroot and goats cheese on top.

  3. Sprinkle crushed walnuts on top.

  4. Drizzle Balsamic Dressing over.
