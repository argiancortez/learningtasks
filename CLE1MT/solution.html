Possible Solutions

  • Support Envrironmental Programs

Educate Children

Educating the children is an act where we should teach the younger generations a more clear explanation on what is right and wrong. Rather than making decisions for themselves, Let's give them a proper amount of education for their future.

Support Environmental Programs

Supporting programs that encourage the cleanliness of our own community makes it easier for us to learn more about proper waste disposal and management. We cannot make a comfortable place to live in if we do not throw our garbage properly.

Prevent Child Marriage

Child Marriage Robs Humans of Their Childhood. Let us stop this kind of practice because early birth and marriage will lead to financial crisis. At a young age, the young should be in school and studying. Be responsible of your own children. Teach them what is wrong and what is right.

Ensure Basic Health Care

Every city or town should have basic health care. It is the local government's job to assure their own people that they have proper health needs if ever an outbreak strikes. Every human life matters no matter what position they have in society. Poor or Rich, they deserve to live.

End Corruption

The impacts of corruption disproportionately affect the most vulnerable people in society. Widespread corruption deters investment, weakens economic growth and undermines the rule of law. Stop corruption now and focus on improving the lives of the poor!
