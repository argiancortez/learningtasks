Basic Pasta

Ingredients

  • Dried pasta.. any will work well

  • Water

  • Salt

  • Tomato Puree

  • Cheese/Tuna/Both/None

Method

  1. Boil your pasta and add salt to the water for later on.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, grate you cheese/drain your tuna.

  3. When the pasta is cooked, add about half a tube of tomato puree, stir well, to give you a Passata Consistency. Of course you can just use passata if you want, and drain the pasta instead

  4. Then, add the tuna if using any, stir it in well. Leave for a minute to heat up the tuna. Add salt/pepper to taste.

  5. Serve into a bowl and sprinke a lot of cheese on top.
